ABOUT ME

I design B2B & SaaS products grounded in data and research. I transform complexity into clarity, working cross-functionally to deliver solutions that drive real business impact, from strategy to scaled outcomes.

WAY OF WORKING

Evidence-Based

Every decision is backed by data and user validation. I don't assume; I investigate. I analyze pathways, behavior through tools like Datadog, and complement with qualitative research.

Strategic Partnership

I work as a partner to the PM and engineering team, not as an executor. Together we balance what's ideal with what's viable, user needs with business objectives.

Outcome-Oriented

I design for measurable impact. Every project has clear success criteria, revenue, speed, retention, or efficiency gains. That's my north star, and it shapes every decision I make.
